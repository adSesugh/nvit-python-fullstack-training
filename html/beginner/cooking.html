How to cook Egusi Soup

Ingredients
  1. Three (3) cups (250mls) of egusi(melon)
  2. One cup (250mls) of palm oil
  3. One cup of crayfish
  4. Stock fish (qty according to ur taste)
  5. 1kg of goat meat
  6. Half kg of kpomo
  7. Locust bean
  8. Okpehe
  9. Ugwu leaf
Steps for frying method
  • Grind the egusi
  • Steam the meat
  • Soak stock fish in a hot water to soften it
